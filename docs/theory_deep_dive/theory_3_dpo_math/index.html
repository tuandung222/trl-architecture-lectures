Phân tích toán học DPO (Direct Preference Optimization)

1. Derivation từ RL Objective​

Bắt đầu từ KL-constrained RL:

max⁡πEx∼DEy∼π(y∣x)[r(x,y)]−βDKL(π(y∣x)∥πref(y∣x))\max_\pi \mathbb{E}_{x \sim \mathcal{D}} \mathbb{E}_{y \sim \pi(y|x)} [r(x,y)] - \beta D_{KL}(\pi(y|x) \| \pi_{ref}(y|x))maxπ​Ex∼D​Ey∼π(y∣x)​[r(x,y)]−βDKL​(π(y∣x)∥πref​(y∣x))

1.1. Optimal policy (closed-form)​

Sử dụng variational calculus, optimal policy có dạng:

π∗(y∣x)=1Z(x)πref(y∣x)exp⁡(r(x,y)β)\pi^*(y|x) = \frac{1}{Z(x)} \pi_{ref}(y|x) \exp\left(\frac{r(x,y)}{\beta}\right)π∗(y∣x)=Z(x)1​πref​(y∣x)exp(βr(x,y)​)

với Z(x)=∑yπref(y∣x)exp⁡(r(x,y)/β)Z(x) = \sum_y \pi_{ref}(y|x) \exp(r(x,y)/\beta)Z(x)=∑y​πref​(y∣x)exp(r(x,y)/β) là partition function.

1.2. Reward reparameterization​

Nghịch đảo công thức trên:

r(x,y)=βlog⁡πθ(y∣x)πref(y∣x)+βlog⁡Z(x)r(x,y) = \beta \log \frac{\pi_\theta(y|x)}{\pi_{ref}(y|x)} + \beta \log Z(x)r(x,y)=βlogπref​(y∣x)πθ​(y∣x)​+βlogZ(x)

Key insight: Reward được biểu diễn qua log probability ratio.

2. Bradley-Terry Preference Model​

Preference data: (x,yw,yl)(x, y_w, y_l)(x,yw​,yl​) với ywy_wyw​ preferred over yly_lyl​.

p(yw≻yl∣x)=exp⁡(r(x,yw))exp⁡(r(x,yw))+exp⁡(r(x,yl))=σ(r(x,yw)−r(x,yl))p(y_w \succ y_l | x) = \frac{\exp(r(x, y_w))}{\exp(r(x, y_w)) + \exp(r(x, y_l))} = \sigma(r(x, y_w) - r(x, y_l))p(yw​≻yl​∣x)=exp(r(x,yw​))+exp(r(x,yl​))exp(r(x,yw​))​=σ(r(x,yw​)−r(x,yl​))

Thay reward bằng reparameterization:

r(x,yw)−r(x,yl)=β(log⁡πθ(yw∣x)πref(yw∣x)−log⁡πθ(yl∣x)πref(yl∣x))r(x, y_w) - r(x, y_l) = \beta\left(\log\frac{\pi_\theta(y_w|x)}{\pi_{ref}(y_w|x)} - \log\frac{\pi_\theta(y_l|x)}{\pi_{ref}(y_l|x)}\right)r(x,yw​)−r(x,yl​)=β(logπref​(yw​∣x)πθ​(yw​∣x)​−logπref​(yl​∣x)πθ​(yl​∣x)​)

Partition function Z(x)Z(x)Z(x) triệt tiêu hoàn toàn, đây là bước đột phá của DPO.

3. DPO Loss​

LDPO(θ)=−E(x,yw,yl)∼D[log⁡σ(βlog⁡πθ(yw∣x)πref(yw∣x)−βlog⁡πθ(yl∣x)πref(yl∣x))]L_{DPO}(\theta) = -\mathbb{E}_{(x, y_w, y_l) \sim \mathcal{D}} \left[\log \sigma\left(\beta \log\frac{\pi_\theta(y_w|x)}{\pi_{ref}(y_w|x)} - \beta \log\frac{\pi_\theta(y_l|x)}{\pi_{ref}(y_l|x)}\right)\right]LDPO​(θ)=−E(x,yw​,yl​)∼D​[logσ(βlogπref​(yw​∣x)πθ​(yw​∣x)​−βlogπref​(yl​∣x)πθ​(yl​∣x)​)]

3.1. Gradient analysis​

∇θLDPO=−β⋅σ(−r^)⋅(∇θlog⁡πθ(yw∣x)−∇θlog⁡πθ(yl∣x))\nabla_\theta L_{DPO} = -\beta \cdot \sigma(-\hat{r}) \cdot \left(\nabla_\theta \log\pi_\theta(y_w|x) - \nabla_\theta \log\pi_\theta(y_l|x)\right)∇θ​LDPO​=−β⋅σ(−r^)⋅(∇θ​logπθ​(yw​∣x)−∇θ​logπθ​(yl​∣x))

với r^=β(log⁡πθ(yw)πref(yw)−log⁡πθ(yl)πref(yl))\hat{r} = \beta(\log\frac{\pi_\theta(y_w)}{\pi_{ref}(y_w)} - \log\frac{\pi_\theta(y_l)}{\pi_{ref}(y_l)})r^=β(logπref​(yw​)πθ​(yw​)​−logπref​(yl​)πθ​(yl​)​).

  • Khi r^\hat{r}r^ lớn (model đã biết phân biệt): gradient nhỏ (converged)
  • Khi r^\hat{r}r^ nhỏ (model chưa phân biệt được): gradient lớn (cần học thêm)

3.2. Implicit reward monotonicity​

DPO đảm bảo implicit reward rθ(x,y)=βlog⁡πθ(y∣x)πref(y∣x)r_\theta(x,y) = \beta \log \frac{\pi_\theta(y|x)}{\pi_{ref}(y|x)}rθ​(x,y)=βlogπref​(y∣x)πθ​(y∣x)​ tăng cho chosen và giảm cho rejected.
