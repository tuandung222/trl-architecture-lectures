Phân tích 8 Loss Types trong GRPOTrainer

1. Standard GRPO (loss_type="grpo")​

L=−1N∑imin⁡(ρiAi,clip(ρi,1−ϵ,1+ϵ)Ai)L = -\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i}\min(\rho_i A_i, \text{clip}(\rho_i, 1-\epsilon, 1+\epsilon)A_i)L=−N1​∑i​min(ρi​Ai​,clip(ρi​,1−ϵ,1+ϵ)Ai​)

Two-sided clipping. Normalization: mean trên completion tokens.

2. DAPO (loss_type="dapo")​

Dynamic Advantage Per-token Optimization: advantage được broadcast per-token thay vì per-sequence.

L=−1∑Mi∑i∑tMi,tmin⁡(ρi,tAi,clip(ρi,t,1−ϵ,1+ϵ)Ai)L = -\frac{1}{\sum M_i}\sum_{i}\sum_t M_{i,t}\min(\rho_{i,t} A_i, \text{clip}(\rho_{i,t}, 1-\epsilon, 1+\epsilon)A_i)L=−∑Mi​1​∑i​∑t​Mi,t​min(ρi,t​Ai​,clip(ρi,t​,1−ϵ,1+ϵ)Ai​)

Normalization: chia cho tổng completion tokens trên toàn batch (thay vì mean per-sample).

3. CISPO (loss_type="cispo")​

Clipped Importance Sampling Policy Optimization. One-sided clipping:

L=−clamp(ρ,max⁡=1+ϵ)⋅A⋅log⁡πθL = -\text{clamp}(\rho, \max=1+\epsilon) \cdot A \cdot \log\pi_\thetaL=−clamp(ρ,max=1+ϵ)⋅A⋅logπθ​

Đặc điểm: Không clip từ dưới, chỉ giới hạn IS ratio từ trên. Dùng gradient của log-prob trực tiếp thay vì ratio x advantage.

4. VESPO (loss_type="vespo")​

Variance-reduced Expected Sequence Policy Optimization với gamma weights:

L=−ϕ(w)⋅A⋅log⁡πθL = -\phi(w) \cdot A \cdot \log\pi_\thetaL=−ϕ(w)⋅A⋅logπθ​

ϕ(w)=eλ⋅wk⋅e−λw\phi(w) = e^\lambda \cdot w^k \cdot e^{-\lambda w}ϕ(w)=eλ⋅wk⋅e−λw

ϕ(1)=1\phi(1) = 1ϕ(1)=1, ϕ(w)\phi(w)ϕ(w) giảm khi www lệch xa 1, giảm variance từ off-policy correction.

5. SAPO (loss_type="sapo")​

Soft Annealed Policy Optimization. Sigmoid thay thế hard clamp:

L=−4τσ(τ(ρ−1))⋅AL = -\frac{4}{\tau}\sigma(\tau(\rho - 1)) \cdot AL=−τ4​σ(τ(ρ−1))⋅A

Temperature τ\tauτ khác nhau cho positive/negative advantages:

  • τpos\tau_{pos}τpos​: sharp sigmoid cho positive advantage (khuyến khích)
  • τneg\tau_{neg}τneg​: gentle sigmoid cho negative advantage (ức chế nhẹ)

6. BNPO (loss_type="bnpo")​

Batch-Normalized PPO. Tương tự GRPO nhưng normalization khác:

A^i=Ai−meanbatch(A)stdbatch(A)+ϵ\hat{A}_i = \frac{A_i - \text{mean}_{batch}(A)}{\text{std}_{batch}(A) + \epsilon}A^i​=stdbatch​(A)+ϵAi​−meanbatch​(A)​

Normalize advantage trên toàn batch thay vì per-group.

7. DR-GRPO (loss_type="dr_grpo")​

Doubly-Robust GRPO. Thêm delta clipping:

ρclipped=clip(ρ,min⁡=0,max⁡=δ)\rho_{clipped} = \text{clip}(\rho, \min=0, \max=\delta)ρclipped​=clip(ρ,min=0,max=δ)

Sau đó áp dụng standard two-sided clipping trên ρclipped\rho_{clipped}ρclipped​. Giảm ảnh hưởng của outlier ratios.

8. LUSPO (loss_type="luspo")​

Length-Unbiased Sequence-level PO. Sequence-level importance sampling:

wseq=exp⁡(1T∑tlog⁡πθ(t)πold(t))w_{seq} = \exp\left(\frac{1}{T}\sum_t \log\frac{\pi_\theta(t)}{\pi_{old}(t)}\right)wseq​=exp(T1​∑t​logπold​(t)πθ​(t)​)

Thay vì token-level IS ratio, dùng geometric mean của ratios trên toàn sequence. Tránh bias từ completion length.
